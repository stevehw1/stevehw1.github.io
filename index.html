What’s Cooking, Good Looking?

Pledge Dinner

Pots. Pans. Shell pasta. Alfredo Sauce. Chicken. The Alpha Zeta Pledge Class was going to cook the best meal ever with these items and ingredients. With jokes and laughter, everyone had a memorable experience playing a role in the cooking whether it was draining the pasta, seasoning the chicken, or washing the dishes. The efficient and collaborative teamwork allowed the pledge class to cook a delicious meal together and grow closer amongst one another.

Spontaneity at its Finest

Pledge Hike

It was pitch dark when the Alpha Zeta Pledge Class packed their bags and slipped on their tennis shoes to head out the door at 5 AM to catch the sunrise. Before their spontaneous team decision, they thought they were going to get a good night’s rest but little did they know, they ended up at Lake Elsinore in Riverside County, about a 40-minute drive away from campus. After driving through a one-lane winding road embedded in the middle of mountains and shrubbery, they were rewarded with the crisp air, warm sunshine, and beautiful scenery.

  • Date: May 4th, 2018

Adventure in the Park

 University Community Park

As collegians faced fun, adventurous challenges to learn survival tactics in the wilderness, their team members cheered with crazy excitement and team spirit. That was exactly what the Alpha Zeta pledge class envisioned at their social event: uncontrollable laughter, great memories, and team-building. From fighting off wild bunnies to learning how to create loud signals, the collegians battled off in four different teams to showcase the skills that they learned for a chance to ultimately win cool prizes.

  • Date: May 11th, 2018

Yakult Factory Tour

 Yakult Factory

Free Yakult samples, cool machinery, and good vibes. The Alpha Zeta pledge class held their professional event at the Yakult Factory in Fountain Valley. Collegians had the opportunity to take a tour and learn about Yakult’s history, special ingredients, and health benefits, and visually spectate its manufacturing process. At the end, the Yakult Factory was kind to pass out little Yakult bottle keychains as a memory of the fascinating tour.

  • Date: May 18th, 2018

Beach Clean Up

 Huntington Beach

Most people go to the beach to swim, sunbathe, and just sit back and relax. However, the Alpha Zeta pledge class and Collegians spent their Saturday morning at Huntington Beach picking up trash and beautifying the area. Afterwards, Vice President of Community Service Aryan Ghanadan taught Collegians about the concept of upcycling, the process of reusing an item to create a better product, and they got to take home cute succulents in jars.

  • Date: May 19th, 2018

A Thousand Miles

Pledge Music Video

Making my way downtown walking fast, faces pass and I’m home bound. The Alpha Zeta pledge class recorded a music video for the nostalgic song “A Thousand Miles” by Vanessa Carlton because it resembles their friendship and measures they’d take for one another. The pledge class had a wonderful time recording footage as they exchanged cringe dance moves and lip-synced to the catchy song.

  • Date: April 28th, 2018
